CV Blog

My name is Seyed. I'm a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at Heriot-Watt University. I'm working in the Semantic Web Lab under the supervision of Dr. Alasdair Gray and Dr. Fiona McNeill. My research currently focuses on Semantic Web, Knowledge Graphs (KGs), and Big Data. More specifically, I'm investigating the quality of the data provenance in KGs.

I did my master's degree in Algorithms and Computing at the Sharif University of Technology. My master's thesis was on Verifiable Secret Sharing. I've been a researcher/developer for more than 4 years, experiencing a wide range of programming platforms and languages (see my CV). Your comments and contributions would be welcome.

BTW, I'm from Ravar, Kerman, Iran. In my free time, I enjoy reading history and playing volleyball.
